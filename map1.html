COVID-19 County Rates (2020)

This map shows county-level COVID-19 case rates across the United States in 2020. Counties are shaded by the number of confirmed cases per 100,000 residents.

Projection: Albers (Equal Area)

Data source:
COVID-19 county-level case data from The New York Times (2020). Population data from the 2018 ACS 5-year estimates. County boundary shapefiles from the U.S. Census Bureau. Data processed for this lab.
